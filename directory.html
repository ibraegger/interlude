(  interlude  )

Brief Pauses of Everyday Motion

This is a digital space for interruption — an archive of twelve interactive moments meant to interrupt routines and invite you to pause, drift, and notice. Each piece is a quiet call to stillness, disrupting the everyday with poetic interludes. Return here anytime to reconnect with a slower rhythm and explore how presence can take many forms.

INT(01)

Sway of Trees

The wind speaks in gestures — slow, unhurried. As the branches sway, they remind us that the world is always moving, even when we forget to feel it. A click opens new windows into stillness.

Video by(Kelly - via Pexels)



INT(02)

Unfinished Correspondence

Some words exist only between thought and disappearance. This page is a space for what doesn’t need to last — a fleeting letter to no one, erased with silence.

Sound design(EgeKazak)

INT(03)

Dance of Breath and Form

Time follows breath. Inhale, hold, exhale, pause. A quiet loop where the body syncs with presence.

Sound design(EgeKazak)

INT(04)

Precipitation as an Art

Rain becomes a language. You shape its density, rhythm, and fall. In this storm, stillness is found between drops..






INT(05)

The Moment a Flower Becomes Itself

Becoming takes time. A flower opens, slowly and fully, revealing the poetry of transformation.

Music(Brian Eno – Bloom: Small World)


INT(06)

The Poem of Unmoving Hands

A poem appears only in stillness. One line at a time, it breathes onto the screen — but vanishes if touched. Some things ask only to be witnessed.

Music(Ryuichi Sakamoto – Walker)

INT(07)

Draw a Pause

A blank space to wander. Every stroke is a breath, made of liquid circles that dry like ink — slowly, silently.

Music(John Cage – Dream)




INT(08)

Spectrum Symphony

Colors fall like sound. A flower grows from water and music, a bloom shaped by melody and movement.

Music(Ryuichi Sakamoto – Disintegration)


INT(09)

Metamorphic Flow

A soft, shifting body, expanding and collapsing. You rotate it, zoom into it — the closer you look, the more it changes.

Music(Brian Eno – Cmon)

INT(10)

Whispers of the Night Sky

A sky for drawing your own stars. Each constellation is a quiet map of your inner landscape.

Music(Ege Kazak - original theme)




INT(11)

A Glitch in the Ordinary

The everyday stutters. A fridge loops. A toothbrush hums strangely. The familiar slips, and you are suddenly awake.

Sound design(Ege Kazak)


INT(12)

Shells Keep Secrets

The ocean distorts what it touches. Pick up the shells — echoes spill out, filtered through water, blurred like memory.

Sound design(Ege Kazak)

Music(Harold Budd - The Pearl)

Interlude is a multidisciplinary project that invites pause in the midst of routine. Through a series of interactive digital experiences, photochromic posters, and an agenda designed to capture moments of stillness, it proposes gentle disruptions to habitual motion.At its core, the project explores how subtle shifts — in time, light, and attention — can awaken presence. It is an index of quiet moments, scattered across media, seasons, and sensations.
Designed, edited, and developed by Team12 as part of KYRT2025 final graduation project at ESAD.
